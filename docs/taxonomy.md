# Taxonomy Structure

SpriteDex follows standard biological taxonomy.

---

# Taxonomic Hierarchy

Kingdom
└── Phylum
    └── Class
        └── Order
            └── Family
                └── Genus
                    └── Species

---

- Fungi
- Insects
- Birds
- Trees
- Fish
